About Our Diabetes Prediction Application

Introduction

This application is designed to help users predict the likelihood of developing diabetes based on various medical factors. We utilize advanced machine learning algorithms to provide accurate and reliable predictions.

Features

  • Predict the risk of diabetes based on user input
  • Provide personalized recommendations based on the prediction
  • Secure user authentication and data management
  • Responsive and user-friendly design for all devices

Team

This application was developed for the Final Year Project, to improve healthcare outcomes.
