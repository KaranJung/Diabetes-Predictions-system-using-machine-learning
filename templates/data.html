Understanding the Inputs

Pregnancies: The number of times the patient has been pregnant.

Glucose: The plasma glucose concentration in mg/dL.

Blood Pressure: The diastolic blood pressure in mmHg.

Skin Thickness: The triceps skin fold thickness in mm.

Insulin: The 2-hour serum insulin in mu U/ml.

BMI: The body mass index in kg/m2.

Diabetes Pedigree Function: A function that scores the likelihood of diabetes based on family history.

Age: The age of the patient in years.
